Our Story

We met in the Cayman Islands back in January 2016 where we had both just joined at Deloitte. Our whole first year Deloitte group went out to dinner that first week and then played Rock n Roll bingo at a local pub where we got to chatting and the rest is history!

Jamie is from Prince Edward Islands (“PEI”) in Canada and before Cayman, went to school in the United States for some of his college years playing tennis and studying before he finished up his CPA qualification (accounting) back in his hometown. Tracey is from Durban, South Africa, went to university in Grahamstown and then finished up her CA qualification (accounting) back in Durban.

Since our “meet cute” in Cayman, we have built a holiday cottage in Rustico, PEI, travelled a little bit of Europe, the US and Cuba along with visiting both our hometowns each year and made it through COVID and lockdowns together (Jamie is a very patient, laid back person).

In December 2019, Jamie got down on one knee and popped the question at Mkuze Falls Private Game Reserve in KZN whilst we were on a drinks stop in the bush. We have been waiting to celebrate our big day with everyone ever since (along with the arrival of our baby in August 2022) and both are finally happening!! Let the countdown to the celebrations begin!
